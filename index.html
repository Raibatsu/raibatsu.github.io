Rawaan

Rawaan

World's first Urdu Dyslexia Application

Rawaan is the first Urdu-based remedial therapy tool for dyslexia. Designed for primary school children, Rawaan delivers an immersive gaming experience that helps improve reading, writing and language skills. With real-time adaptation, Rawaan presents an increasingly challenging environment, making learning an exciting journey. Suitable for children who struggle with the Urdu language or use it as a second language, Rawaan is the perfect tool for building a strong foundation in Urdu literacy.
